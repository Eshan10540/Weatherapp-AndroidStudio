Features

Current Weather: View current weather conditions including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.
Location-Based: Automatically detects the user's location or allows manual location input.
Weather Alerts: Receive weather alerts and notifications for severe weather conditions.
Customization: Personalize the app with different themes and backgrounds.

Usage

Upon opening the app, Weather App will automatically detect your location and display the current weather conditions.
Swipe left or right to navigate between the hourly and daily forecasts.
Tap on the menu icon to access additional features such as location search, settings, and weather alerts.
Customize the app settings according to your preferences, including units, themes, and notification preferences.
